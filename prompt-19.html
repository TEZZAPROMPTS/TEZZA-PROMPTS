Ultra-realistic cinematic female portrait — Blender Cycles render
Camera & composition

Vertical medium-close shot, framed from upper bust to head. Top-down angle as if captured in a mirror selfie while the subject is lying down. Camera positioned slightly above the face, angled downward. Intimate, natural framing. Subject centered. Shallow depth of field with background texture still visible.

Face & identity (reference-based, do not alter)

Face must strictly match the provided female reference image. Absolute identity preservation: same facial structure, proportions, lips, nose, eyes, eyebrows, cheek volume and jawline.
Relaxed, soft, confident expression. Slightly parted lips. Calm gaze directed toward the camera. No facial modification or beautification.

Hair (modified — loose, natural, blonde)

Long hair fully loose and naturally spread across the surface beneath the subject, softly fanned out around the head and shoulders, with organic irregularity.
Hair color: natural blonde, warm-neutral tone (not platinum, not artificial), with subtle tonal variation and slightly darker roots for realism.
Visible individual strands, soft flyaways, gentle bends and natural gravity behavior. No symmetry. No styling. No excessive shine.
Ultra-realistic Blender Cycles hair simulation with physically accurate shaders.

Body & pose (reference-based)

Natural feminine body proportions preserved. One hand gently touching near the lips with relaxed fingers. Shoulders and upper chest relaxed. Pose identical to the reference image.

Outfit & accessories

Simple white sleeveless top with thin straps, naturally fitted. Realistic fabric texture with subtle folds.
Gold accessories preserved exactly as reference: layered delicate necklaces (including cross pendant), gold bracelet and gold wristwatch. Realistic metal reflections and weight.

Skin realism

Warm light-to-medium skin tone. Ultra-detailed realistic skin texture with visible pores, fine freckles, natural redness on cheeks and nose, subtle imperfections. Accurate subsurface scattering. Natural skin sheen, no plastic or doll effect.

Lighting

Soft, diffused natural indoor lighting. Balanced exposure. Gentle highlights on face, lips, collarbone and jewelry. No harsh shadows.

Environment

White textured bedspread filling the background. Fabric weave clearly visible, soft and realistic. Clean, minimal setting.

Mood & aesthetic

Intimate, effortless, natural beauty. Calm and confident feminine presence. Lifestyle realism with subtle sensuality.

Ultra-photorealistic 3D realism. Blender Cycles render style. HDR, 8K resolution. Sharp focus on face, hair strands, skin texture, fabric and jewelry. Physically accurate materials and lighting.
