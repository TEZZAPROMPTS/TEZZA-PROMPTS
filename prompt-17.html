Ultra-realistic cinematic female portrait — Hyper-realistic Sims Alpha Render (Photorealism)
Camera & composition

Vertical medium-close shot, framed from mid-torso to head. Mirror selfie composition with the subject holding the camera slightly above chest level, angled subtly downward. Three-quarter frontal angle, torso facing forward, head gently tilted. Camera preserves natural human proportions and realistic lens compression (50–70mm equivalent). Subject centered with an intimate, editorial composition. Shallow depth of field with soft, realistic background blur.

Face & identity (reference-based, do not alter)

Face must strictly match the provided female facial reference image. Preserve exact facial anatomy, proportions, cheek volume, jaw softness, lips, nose bridge, nostrils, eyebrows, eyelids, eye spacing and eye shape.
No facial redesign, no beautification, no Sims exaggeration.
Natural, calm and confident expression identical to the reference. Realistic micro-expressions, subtle asymmetry preserved. Natural gaze directed toward the camera.

Hair (hyper-realistic alpha — color adjusted)

Hairstyle must exactly replicate the reference image structure. Medium-length textured curls styled into two small space buns on top of the head, with loose curls framing the face and falling naturally over the shoulders.
Uniform blonde hair color (solid blonde, no gradient, no ombré).
Ultra-high-density alpha hair with fine individual strands, realistic clumping, natural flyaways and irregular strand thickness. Physically believable softness and volume. No plastic shine, no Sims-style gloss.

Body & physique (human realism, no Sims exaggeration)

Natural feminine physique matching the reference proportions. Realistic bust volume, soft shoulders, natural arms. One hand gently resting against the cheek, the other extended holding the phone. Relaxed posture conveying intimacy and realism. Human-accurate anatomy and skin deformation. No stylization.

Outfit & accessories (reference-accurate, unchanged)

Light yellow sleeveless top with deep V neckline and front tie detail, identical to the reference.
Ultra-realistic fabric simulation with visible weave texture, natural folds, gravity-based draping and subtle fabric tension. Delicate necklace with a small heart pendant. No additional accessories.

Skin & realism (photorealistic alpha skin)

Light skin tone with warm bronze tan, evenly distributed.
Ultra-realistic skin texture with visible pores, subtle redness, soft freckles, natural under-eye depth and micro color variation. Physically correct subsurface scattering.
Natural skin sheen — no blur, no plastic, no doll effect.

Lighting

Soft natural daylight entering the room combined with gentle ambient indoor lighting. Realistic light falloff, soft shadows under chin and collarbones. Subtle highlights on cheekbones, nose bridge and shoulders. Neutral-warm white balance. No game-like lighting.

Environment

Realistic bedroom interior inspired by the reference. Pastel walls, bed with soft textiles, anime posters visible but naturally integrated. Objects maintain realistic scale, depth and perspective. Background softly blurred with true optical depth, not artificial blur.

Mood & aesthetic

Natural, intimate, modern feminine presence. Calm, confident and authentic mood. Lifestyle portrait that feels photographed, not rendered.

Style & render quality

Hyper-realistic 3D realism.
The Sims used only as a technical base — final result indistinguishable from a real photo.
HDR, ultra-high resolution, razor-sharp facial, skin, hair and fabric detail. Physically accurate lighting and materials. Zero CGI look.
