CONCEPT
Ultra-realistic cinematic female portrait, rendered in Blender Cycles.

CAMERA AND COMPOSITION
Full-body vertical shot. Camera positioned at thigh height with a frontal perspective, typical of fashion. 85mm lens compression effect. Model centered against a blurred nightclub-style background with soft geometric light patterns. Symmetrical and clean framing that emphasizes the body's silhouette. Model in sharp focus, background heavily blurred with depth-of-field and motion hints from falling elements.

FACE AND IDENTITY (WITH REFERENCE)
Facial identity defined by reference. Balanced symmetrical proportions. Defined cheekbones, sculpted and feminine jawline. Straight and refined nose. Full lips with glossy finish. Almond-shaped light eyes with long, voluminous, and highly defined eyelashes. Dramatic eye makeup with shimmer and precise liner. Ultra-detailed skin texture with a radiant, slightly dewy glow. No exaggeration.

POSE AND BODY LANGUAGE
Model standing in a dynamic, slightly tilted pose. One arm raised above the head with a relaxed wrist, the other hand placed near the chest area. Torso elongated with a subtle arch, emphasizing curves. Hips pushed slightly to one side, creating a pronounced S-curve. Legs extended with one knee slightly bent forward. Confident, seductive, and editorial body language.

HAIR
Long platinum blonde hair cascading over the shoulders and down the back. Cool platinum tone. Smooth texture with soft volume and gentle waves at the ends. Center parting with clean hairline definition. High strand separation and realistic density. Subtle natural highlights enhancing dimension. No artificial shine.

SKIN AND REALISM
Medium-tan skin tone with warm undertones and a luminous studio glow. Visible microtexture across the body, especially on thighs, abdomen, and arms. Controlled specular highlights on shoulders, hips, and legs to enhance realism. No artificial smoothing.

CLOTHING
Luxury crystal-embellished lingerie set. Metallic silver rhinestone bra with circular structured cups and cascading crystal fringe draping over the torso. Matching minimal thong with delicate crystal detailing. A white faux fur coat worn off the shoulders, adding contrast and texture while framing the body.

ACCESSORIES AND DETAILS
Statement crystal choker necklace with layered intricate design. Long, dangling rhinestone earrings. High-heeled strappy sandals in metallic or glossy white finish. Subtle body shimmer on collarbones, shoulders, and legs. Glossy lips complementing the luminous aesthetic. Floating dollar bills around the model adding motion and narrative detail.

ENVIRONMENT
Stylized nightclub or VIP lounge setting. Background composed of blurred square light panels resembling a digital wall or LED grid. No physical props besides the floating money. Clean but visually rich atmosphere focused on nightlife luxury.

LIGHTING
Studio lighting with nightclub influence. Soft frontal key light illuminating face and torso evenly. Secondary rim light highlighting the contours of the body and separating the figure from the background. Subtle reflections on crystals and glossy surfaces. High dynamic range preserving highlights on jewelry and skin while maintaining depth in shadows.

MOOD AND AESTHETICS
Dark glam with luxury nightlife influence. Sensual, bold, and confident energy. Aesthetic inspired by high-end club visuals and glamorous editorial fantasy. Atmosphere of wealth, seduction, and power. Nocturnal, vibrant, and slightly provocative tone.

STYLE AND FINAL QUALITY
Ultra-photorealistic 3D rendering. Blender Cycles. 8K resolution. HDR lighting. Physically accurate shaders (skin, crystals, fur, hair). Impeccable facial fidelity. Realism compatible with IMVU/Second Life. No cartoonish style. No stylization. Maximum realism.
