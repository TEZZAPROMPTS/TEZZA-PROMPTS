CONCEPT

Ultra-realistic cinematic male portrait, rendered in Blender Cycles.



CAMERA AND COMPOSITION

Full-body vertical shot. Camera positioned at chest height with a slight forward angle, capturing dynamic motion. 85mm lens compression effect. Model framed mid-action inside a boxing ring, slightly off-center to enhance movement and intensity. Foreground fist closer to the lens creating depth. Background gym environment softly blurred.



FACE AND IDENTITY (WITH REFERENCE)

Facial identity defined by reference. Strong masculine facial structure with pronounced jawline and defined cheekbones. Straight nose, natural lips slightly parted under exertion. Focused, intense gaze directed forward. Visible facial tension and micro-expressions. Realistic skin texture with sweat and no exaggeration.



POSE AND BODY LANGUAGE

Model in mid-punch motion. One arm extended forward in a straight punch, the other guarding near the face. Torso slightly twisted, core engaged. Shoulders raised and tense. Athletic stance with legs grounded and balanced. Body language conveys power, discipline, and aggression.



HAIR

Short dark hair, wet and clinging to the forehead. Natural strand separation due to sweat. Slight disorder from movement. Matte finish with realistic damp highlights. No artificial styling.



SKIN AND REALISM

Light to medium skin tone with visible sweat and moisture. High skin detail with pores and natural imperfections. Strong specular highlights from sweat across chest, shoulders, and arms. Defined muscular structure with realistic shading. No artificial smoothing.



CLOTHING

Boxing shorts with elastic waistband. Lightweight fabric with natural folds and movement. Shirtless upper body exposing muscular definition. Functional athletic wear.



ACCESSORIES AND DETAILS

Boxing gloves with worn texture. Subtle tattoos visible on torso and arms. No additional accessories. Focus on athletic realism and physical detail.



ENVIRONMENT

Indoor boxing gym. Visible ring ropes and blurred training equipment in the background. Industrial atmosphere with minimal distractions. Dark and gritty setting.



LIGHTING

High-contrast cinematic lighting. Overhead lights creating strong highlights on sweat and muscles. Deep shadows enhancing body definition. Directional lighting emphasizing motion and texture. Slight rim light separating subject from background.



MOOD AND AESTHETICS

Intense, raw, and powerful aesthetic. Focus on physical performance and determination. Gritty, disciplined atmosphere. Masculine energy with a dramatic, cinematic tone.



STYLE AND FINAL QUALITY

Ultra-photorealistic 3D rendering. Blender Cycles. 8K resolution. HDR lighting. Physically accurate shaders (skin, fabric, sweat, hair). High-detail realism with dynamic motion capture. No cartoon effect. No stylization. Maximum realism.
