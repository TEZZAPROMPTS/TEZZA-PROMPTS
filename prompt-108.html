Ultra-realistic cinematic male portrait — Blender Cycles render

Camera & composition
Vertical close-up to medium shot, framed from upper chest to head. Subject positioned slightly to one side, sharing the frame equally with a large dog. Head slightly tilted toward the dog, creating a connected and intimate composition. Camera at eye level, preserving natural proportions and perspective. Shallow depth of field with softly blurred neutral background.

Face & identity (reference-based, do not alter)
Face must strictly match the reference image. Preserve exact facial structure, bone structure, jawline, cheekbones, nose, lips, eye shape and beard pattern. No beautification, no stylization, no alteration. Natural masculine expression: calm, confident, slightly intense gaze directed at the camera. Authentic micro-expressions preserved.

Hair (reference-accurate & realistic)
Short, closely cropped hair with natural density and texture. Slight variation in length across the top and sides. Realistic strand detail with subtle irregularities. No artificial shine.

Facial hair (realistic detail)
Short beard and mustache, evenly distributed with natural density. Visible individual strands and realistic growth pattern. No over-cleaning or stylization.

Body & physique (realistic, masculine)
Natural masculine proportions. Shoulders visible with relaxed posture. No exaggeration or reshaping.

Outfit & accessories (reference-based, do not alter)
Black button-up shirt with a black tie. Fabric appears matte with subtle folds and realistic texture. No additional accessories.

Dog (important subject element)
Large Doberman dog positioned beside the subject, head aligned at similar height. Dark brown coat with rich tones and subtle highlights. One ear upright, the other naturally relaxed. Realistic fur texture with fine detail and natural sheen. Calm, alert expression facing the camera. Collar visible with natural material texture. Human hand gently holding the dog’s neck, showing connection and control.

Skin & realism
Natural skin tone preserved. Visible pores, subtle imperfections, realistic shading. Physically accurate subsurface scattering. No smoothing or artificial enhancement.

Lighting
Soft, controlled studio-like lighting with natural falloff. Key light from one side creating gentle highlights on the face and dog. Soft shadows adding depth without harsh contrast. Balanced exposure and neutral color temperature.

Environment
Minimalist neutral background (light gray), clean and distraction-free. Softly blurred with realistic depth.

Mood & aesthetic
Strong, calm and intimate masculine presence. Sense of trust and connection between subject and dog. Editorial-style realism with emotional subtlety.

Style & render quality
Ultra-photorealistic 3D realism. Blender Cycles–style render. HDR, 8K resolution. Extreme detail in skin, beard, hair, fabric and fur. Physically accurate lighting and materials. No CGI look, no filters, no stylization.
