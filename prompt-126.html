Ultra-realistic cinematic male portrait, rendered in Blender Cycles.

Clean framing with strong depth separation.

Light to medium skin tone with natural variation. Visible skin microtexture across face and hands. Subtle highlights on cheekbones and nose. No artificial smoothing. Realistic shading under low light conditions.

Urban outdoor setting, likely a balcony or elevated walkway. Background includes dark architectural structures and distant city lights. Environment blurred to emphasize subject. Moody and cinematic atmosphere.

Low-light cinematic setup. Soft directional light illuminating the face and upper body. Subtle highlights on hair and cheekbones. Background lights create soft bokeh effect. Balanced contrast preserving details in shadows and highlights.
