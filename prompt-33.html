Ultra-realistic cinematic female portrait — Blender Cycles render

Camera & composition
Vertical close-up portrait, framed from upper chest to top of the head. Three-quarter frontal angle with the face slightly turned toward the camera. Camera positioned at eye level, tight composition, intimate framing. Shallow depth of field with soft background falloff. Identical pose, angle and proportions to the reference image.

Face & identity fidelity
Facial structure, bone anatomy, expression, nose, lips, jawline and symmetry strictly identical to the reference image. No facial alteration, no stylization, no beautification changes.

Eyes (REFERENCE-LOCKED)
Eyes must strictly match the reference image in shape, size, spacing, eyelid fold, iris color and pupil size. Natural gaze, intense and calm expression preserved. Realistic eye moisture, sharp iris detail, natural reflections consistent with the lighting.

Skin & texture
Ultra-detailed realistic skin with visible pores, fine texture, natural highlights on cheekbones and nose bridge. Subtle freckles and micro-imperfections preserved. No smoothing, no plastic effect.

Lips & makeup
Full lips with deep matte mauve/burgundy lipstick, soft gradient edges. Defined brows, elegant eyeliner and subtle eyeshadow. Sophisticated, high-end makeup finish.

Hair
Blonde hair (color change only), maintaining the same hairstyle, volume, parting and flow as the reference.
Loose strands falling naturally across the forehead and partially over one eye, with additional fine flyaway hairs framing the face.
Hair appears soft, dense and realistic, with natural strand separation and organic movement.

Clothing & accessories
Black deep V-neck top, elegant and minimal. Delicate gold necklace layered at the collarbone. Small stud earrings. Beauty mark on the chest preserved.

Lighting
Cinematic low-key lighting with soft directional light from the front/side, creating depth and gentle shadows. Dark, moody background with smooth gradient falloff.

Mood & aesthetic
Elegant, sensual, editorial beauty portrait. High-fashion, intimate and cinematic realism.

Rendering style & quality
Ultra-realistic Blender Cycles render, HDR, 8K resolution, razor-sharp facial focus, realistic skin shader with subsurface scattering. IMVU / Second Life realism reference.

Negative prompt
Eye color change, altered eye shape, facial distortion, stylized face, cartoon, anime, painterly look, beauty filter, over-smooth skin, plastic texture, low resolution, blur, unrealistic hair symmetry.
