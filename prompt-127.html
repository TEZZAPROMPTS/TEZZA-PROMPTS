CONCEPT
Ultra-realistic cinematic male portrait, rendered in Blender Cycles.

CAMERA AND COMPOSITION
Full-body vertical shot. Camera positioned from the back passenger seat at chest height, angled slightly forward toward the driver. 85mm lens compression effect. Framing captures the model seated in the driver’s seat, turned partially toward the camera. Interior elements create natural framing lines. Background desert landscape visible through windshield, softly blurred.

FACE AND IDENTITY (WITH REFERENCE)
Facial identity defined by reference. Strong masculine facial structure with a sharp jawline and defined cheekbones. Straight nose and naturally full lips. Almond-shaped eyes with an intense, confident gaze directed toward the camera. Skin retains realistic texture and natural tonal variation. No exaggeration.

POSE AND BODY LANGUAGE
Model seated in the driver’s seat. One hand resting on the steering wheel, the other near the center console. Upper body slightly twisted toward the camera. Shoulders relaxed but controlled. Head turned back, maintaining eye contact. Body language conveys confidence, control, and calm authority.

HAIR
Short dark hair, styled neatly with a natural side part. Slight volume at the top with controlled strands. Clean edges with realistic density. Subtle highlights from sunlight. Natural finish with no artificial shine.

SKIN AND REALISM
Light to medium skin tone with warm sunlight reflecting across the face. Visible microtexture and natural shading. Soft highlights on cheekbones and forehead. No artificial smoothing. Realistic interaction with ambient light.

CLOTHING
Black leather jacket with a fitted silhouette. Subtle texture with natural creases and reflections. Dark inner clothing, minimal and understated. Clean, modern styling.

ACCESSORIES AND DETAILS
Minimal accessories. Vehicle interior details visible including steering wheel, dashboard screens, and center console interface. No excessive personal accessories.

ENVIRONMENT
Interior of a modern luxury car. Clean dashboard with digital displays. Panoramic windshield showing an open desert landscape with distant mountains. Minimalist and premium environment.

LIGHTING
Golden hour natural lighting. Warm sunlight entering through windshield, creating soft highlights and lens flare. Balanced exposure with gentle shadows inside the car. Natural cinematic glow across face and interior surfaces.

MOOD AND AESTHETICS
Luxury lifestyle aesthetic. Calm, confident, and slightly mysterious tone. Sense of freedom and control. Cinematic atmosphere with warm, aspirational energy.

STYLE AND FINAL QUALITY
Ultra-photorealistic 3D rendering. Blender Cycles. 8K resolution. HDR lighting. Physically accurate shaders (skin, leather, glass, metal). High-detail realism in both subject and environment. No cartoon effect. No stylization. Maximum realism.
